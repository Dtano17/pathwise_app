## 3. Full Description (max 4000 chars)

```
The everyday planner that does more than plan — it keeps you accountable.

See a recipe on Instagram? A workout on TikTok? A travel guide on YouTube? Share it to JournalMate and AI instantly turns it into YOUR personalized, actionable plan. Set reminders, sync to your calendar, and never miss a thing.

BUILT FOR EVERYDAY PLANNERS
Whether you're planning a Thanksgiving dinner, prepping for a job interview, or organizing a weekend trip — JournalMate turns your ideas into action. No more bookmarking and forgetting. Plan it, track it, crush it.

SHARE FROM ANYWHERE — YOUR SUPERPOWER
JournalMate is the only planner that lets you grab content from any app and own it:
• Share an Instagram Reel → Get a step-by-step meal plan with budget
• Share a TikTok workout → Get a personalized fitness routine
• Share a YouTube travel vlog → Get a complete itinerary with costs
• Paste a ChatGPT conversation → Get organized action items
• Share any URL → AI extracts everything and builds your plan

Our AI reads audio, captions, images (OCR), and text to extract venues, prices, ingredients, exercises, and destinations — then structures it into tasks you can track and complete.

COPY COMMUNITY PLANS
Browse hundreds of plans shared by real people — Thanksgiving Feast ($320), Christmas Celebration ($850), Shopping Guides, NYE Party Plans, and more. See what's trending near you. Preview any plan and add it with one tap. Customize it, make it yours.

NEVER MISS OUT — REMINDERS & CALENDAR SYNC
• Set smart reminders for every task
• Auto-sync to Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or Outlook
• Get AI-powered timing suggestions (e.g., "Book now — prices drop in 3 days")
• See trending plans near your location so you never miss local events

PLAN FOR OTHERS & EARN REWARDS
Create plans for friends and family. Share your Thanksgiving menu, your workout routine, or your travel itinerary with the community. The more you plan and share:
• Earn badges: Task Rookie, Task Pro, Perfectionist, Social Butterfly, Group Leader, Planner, and 14 more
• Track streaks and climb the leaderboard
• Get recognized as a top planner in your community

COLLABORATE WITH GROUPS
Create groups for shared goals — Thanksgiving planning with family, fitness challenges with friends, book clubs, travel squads. See everyone's progress and achieve goals together.

KEY FEATURES
✦ AI Planning: Quick Plan (instant) or Smart Plan (personalized)
✦ Share-to-Plan: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, ChatGPT, Claude, any URL
✦ Smart Extraction: AI reads audio, captions, images, and text
✦ Community Plans: Browse & use plans shared by others
✦ Reminders & Calendar Sync: Never miss an event
✦ Trending Near You: See what plans are popular in your area
✦ Task Management: Complete, snooze, schedule, archive
✦ Groups & Collaboration: Plan together, track team progress
✦ Badges & Streaks: Earn 20+ achievement badges
✦ Personal Journal: Track movies, books, restaurants, experiences
✦ Voice Input: Speak your goals naturally
✦ AI Recommendations: Tips, costs, and venue suggestions per task

PLAN ANYTHING
★ Weekend trips & travel itineraries
★ Date nights & romantic experiences
★ Workout routines & health goals
★ Thanksgiving dinners & holiday parties
★ Career development & interview prep
★ Home projects & family activities
★ Financial planning & meal prep

PRIVACY FIRST
Your data stays yours. Plans and journal entries are private by default. Share only what you choose.

Stop bookmarking. Start owning. Download JournalMate today.
```
