## ‚öñÔ∏è **LEGAL CONSIDERATIONS**

### **Priority Date Mechanics:**

In a CIP, you get **TWO priority dates:**

1. **Original Provisional Date** applies to:
   - Badge categorization
   - Theme-based filtering
   - Peer-to-peer sharing
   - Social media redistribution (sharing out)
   - Monetization strategies

2. **CIP Filing Date** applies to:
   - URL-to-plan extraction
   - Multi-modal processing
   - Platform-specific strategies
   - Share sheet integration

**Why This Matters:**
- Earlier priority date = better protection against competitors
- If someone filed a similar URL-to-plan system between your provisional date and CIP date, they could potentially block your claims
- **Speed matters** - file CIP ASAP to get earliest possible priority date for new features

### **What Happens to Your Provisional:**

Once you file the CIP:
- ‚úÖ Provisional is incorporated into CIP application
- ‚úÖ Original priority date preserved for old claims
- ‚úÖ Provisional officially expires (no longer needed)
- ‚úÖ CIP becomes the active application
